Introduction

In today’s digital world, the way we communicate online shapes how others perceive us. Being responsible online means thinking before posting, respecting others, and protecting your personal information.

Respectful Communication

Responsible online communication involves using polite language, avoiding cyberbullying, and being careful with jokes and comments. Remember that once something is online, it can stay there forever.

Online Identity

Your online identity is how people see you on social media, forums, or websites. Protect your identity by:

  • Using strong passwords
  • Thinking before sharing personal info
  • Setting privacy settings
  • Being aware of your digital footprint
